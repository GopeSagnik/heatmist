Why KoolCity

Dual impact — Heat & Pollution

Kolkata summers are brutal. But rising temperature isn't the only threat — particulate matter and dust make every breath a burden. Mist tackles both.

🌡️
Instant Cooling Effect

Fine water droplets evaporate rapidly, absorbing ambient heat and lowering the surrounding air temperature by 6–10°C in under 2 minutes. Tested in tropical conditions similar to Kolkata's climate.

💨
Traps Airborne Dust

Mist particles bind to PM2.5 and PM10 pollutants, pulling them to ground level — up to 60% reduction in dust near sprayer zones.

🚌
Bus Stand First

Bus stand commuters bear the worst of Kolkata's heat. Our first installations target high-footfall stops, where even a few minutes of relief matters most.

💧
Water Efficient

Ultra-fine nozzles use a fraction of water compared to traditional sprinklers — optimised for maximum cooling per litre.

🌱
Zero Carbon Footprint

Our systems run on low-power motors and can be paired with solar. Misting doesn't burn fuel — it reverses the effect of those that do.
